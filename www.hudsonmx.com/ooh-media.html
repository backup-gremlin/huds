Customizable Solution for Modern OOH Buying, enabled by Hudson MX.

With your new solution configured by Hudson MX, it doesn’t take a village or an overnight wait to ensure that systems are up to date and in sync. There’s no more need for unnecessary workarounds that may have gotten you and your team through the day in the past — from planning around system shutdowns to reliance on batch versus on-demand processes to enlisting a manager just to check status. Whatever data fields and reports you need will be accurate, up to date, and at your fingertips — not lagging behind your clients’ expectations. 

Whether you do your end-to-end buying in BuyerAssist™ or use it as a robust reconciliation and/or reporting supplement to your existing buying tool, you’ll enjoy unprecedented visibility and peace of mind. Your custom solution will free you up to do less worrying about lags and errors and more of what you do best: developing insights, testing new strategies, and crafting campaigns that drive top-notch results for your brands.

 
 

The MX Platform™ for
OOH at a glance

The platform is custom-configured to support desired workflows.

 

  • Enhanced Visibility

    View live financial data and buy status — reduce reliance on managers as “go-betweens” for routine tasks.

  • Modern-Day Flexibility

    Easily access any data you need for reporting and invoice reconciliation — no more artificial limitations on number and type of data fields that can be shared across platforms.

    Gain ability to change orders and match invoices whenever you want, as many times as needed, without the constraints of batch processing.

    Gain anywhere, anytime access to your cloud-based solution.

    Customize your reports with the fields and formats you want.

  • Greater Accuracy and Reliability

    Get instant notification of any sync errors.
