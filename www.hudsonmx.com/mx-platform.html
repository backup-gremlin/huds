Meet the MX Platform

Discover the Power of MX

Flexible omnichannel media planning, buying, billing, and paying for the agencies and brands leading the way

Media tech for what’s next… and now.

MX Platform is the world’s first and only fully cloud-based centralized media management platform designed to empower efficiency, control, and innovation at enterprise scale.

Simplify & enhance every stage of media activation

Oversee, execute, track & optimize media investments the easy way.

Expand a section to see how we can help

  • Streamline campaign and flight planning, with systematic approvals.

    Plan and forecast for all channels & platforms within a single workflow.

    Set up your own custom approval “gates” at key points in the activation lifecycle.

    Initiate orders and revisions in one centralized place for all vendors and media (if negotiating directly with sellers).

  • Easily see budgeted, actual, and invoiced spend across all campaigns in real time

    Automatically see and act upon live performance data across integrated vendors like Google Ads, Bing, Facebook, Google DV360, and Google Campaign Manager (GCM).

    Use auto-actualization based on your unique business logic—calculated and sent to vendors.

  • Easily see budgeted, actual, and invoiced spend across all campaigns in real time.

    Centrally create and approve campaign budget to eliminate unauthorized spend.

    If you bill clients, leverage flexible billing rules engine to create consolidated bills across all media and fee categories while supporting any desired commercial structure.

    Enable rules-based invoice reconciliation—including all fees and live spend data.

    Increase accuracy and control over who gets paid when.

    Accurately and automatically record media transactions in your General Ledger at any level of detail and frequency.
