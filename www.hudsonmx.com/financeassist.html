FinanceAssist™

 
 

Streamline media finance operations—automated invoice reconciliation, faster billing & paying, cross-channel billing & reporting, and more

 

Media-related finance work doesn’t have to be slow, tedious, error-prone or a burden on buying teams. It simply takes the right modern, media-specific tools. The FinanceAssist module of the MX Platform seamlessly connects all your internal and external data, tools, workflows, and teams in one central place…then applies your own custom automation rules. You’ll get greater insight and control—without unnecessary hassles, delays, or mundane, repetitive work.

Book a Demo
 
 

You may need FinanceAssist if your team…

  1. Keys in data that feels like it could be auto-populated for you

  2. Struggles with real-time visibility into spend vs. plan

  3. Isn’t always sure which media vendor invoices to pay when

  4. Manually matches up every vendor invoice to corresponding orders

  5. Performs repetitive and error-prone calculations “by-hand”

  6. Creates a bunch of extra bills instead of one unified bill for all media

  7. Waits overnight for batch processing of bills, reports…or anything

  8. Manually creates cross-media reports in spreadsheets

 
 
 
 

FinanceAssist™ seamlessly connects you with the people, partners, data, and tools you work with every day.

 
 
 

Media accounting, made faster and easier.

  • Convenience and Time Savings

    Automating reconciliation processes

    Seeing status of buys and spend on-demand—24/7.

    Generating a single bill for all media types.

  • Greater Insight into the Big Picture

    Gaining unprecedented visibility with real-time, cross-media reporting.

    Accessing a real-time unified view of spend metrics across all media types and channels.

  • Enhanced Control and Risk Management

    Leveraging fully configurable invoice matching, client billing rules, and bill design uniformity.

    Easily controlling spend and preventing overspending.

  • Greater Efficacy and Job Satisfaction
