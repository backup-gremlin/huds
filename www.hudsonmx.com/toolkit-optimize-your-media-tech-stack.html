Optimizing your in-house media tech stack

According to a 2023 study by the ANA, 54% of in-house agencies handle some media planning/buying services. One-third have some in-house programmatic capabilities.

Whether you have an established omni-digital, in-house media buying workflowor youre simply looking to track, reconcile, optimize, or pay for your media buys with your agency partnersuse this resource toolkit to ensure you have the tools and processes in place to reducing risk, inefficiency, and errors.


Whats inside:

  • Checklists to ensure that your media workflow is powered with the technical functionality you need to ensure media spend visibility, budget control, and data-driven optimization. 

  • In-house and co-housing cost assessment sheet

  • Roadmap and insights from major in-house advertising teams for scalable in-housing and co-housing strategies
