Hudson MX Advance Program: Empower Your Career with a Global Technology Company

We cant wait to meet you!

Looking to launch a new career path as you help grow and scale a global technology company? Hudson MX Advance is a full-time employment and career acceleration program for entry-level professionals looking to take their careers and skills to the next level.

 
 

Program highlights

This one-year program is designed to help entry-level professionals accelerate career development, discover their passions, and gain the experience and tools crucial to successwherever your career takes you. The Hudson MX Advance program will also provide participants opportunities for coaching, leadership development, mentorship, and opportunities to work with colleagues at every level.

Our company is at an inflection point as we embark on the journey to implement and commercialize a product suite that has been years in the making. Hudson MX Advance participants will be business builders at this critical juncture. Were looking for empaths...doers...glass-half-fullersand most importantlysomeone who wed have fun sitting next to on a long bus ride.

We cant wait to meet you!

  • Personalized Career Development

  • Build relationships with experienced mentors

  • Learn about other departments with special cross-team projects

  • Opportunity to supercharge your career in a fast-growing startup

  • Be a part of a global client + colleague ecosystem

  • Speakers series, lunch-and-learns, & happy hours

FAQs

Where is the program available?

While our offices are in Atlanta and New York City, we are open to employees working remotely from across the United States.

Who is ideal the candidate?

We are looking for teammates who welcome new challenges and are comfortable assuming a great deal of responsibility early on in their careers. Youll thrive in this role if youre someone who embraces ambiguity and has a passion for solving problems.

Do I need specific skills or media industry experience to apply?

Participants will need to be detail-oriented, responsive, and adaptive. As our business grows, our global clients will require a level of service that is unparalleled and consistently high quality. Our teams need a diversity of thought to be successful, so were looking for people with different backgrounds, skills, and experiences.

How long is this program?

Weve structured the program to last approximately 12-months for participants who accomplish milestones within targeted timelines.

Is this a full-time employment opportunity?

Yes, this program is a full-time employment opportunity. All Hudson MX Advance participants are eligible for our full package of employee benefits, including competitive salary, unlimited PTO; employer-covered medical, dental, and vision coverage; 401k; paid parental leave; and employer-sponsored continuing education opportunities.
