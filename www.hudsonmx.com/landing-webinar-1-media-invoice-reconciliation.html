Low Touch Digital Media Invoice Reconciliation

In this MX Platform™ Feature Spotlight, our Hudson MX expert team will share an overview of our powerful low-touch invoice reconciliation functionality and how our brand and agency clients are using it to reduce burdens for media teams, keep buyers focused on planning, buying, and closing out campaigns, saving time, and reducing errors.

    • How to spend no more than 5 minutes per media invoice and electronically process at least 40% of all media invoices.

    • The benefits of having a single source of truth for all your invoices across different media channels.

    • How to use rules-based matching and automation to save time and minimize errors.

    • The importance of auditability and cash flow insights in modern payment systems.
