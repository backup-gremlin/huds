Podcasts to Stay on Top of the Latest Digital Media and Advertising Trends

Sep 9
Written By Laura Franta-Abdalla

Keeping up with the latest trends and updates from across the advertising ecosystem can be a challenging task in itself. But where’s the time?

Recently, we gathered a few favorite industry podcasts from colleagues and clients. From what’s next from ad platforms like Google and Amazon to who has the top-performing campaigns this season, these easy listens provide insights into the evolving landscape of digital advertising, technological innovations, and what’s next for the industry.

Take a look at our podcast roundup to stay ahead of the curve.

Marketecture
Hosted by Ari Paparo and Eric Franchi
Looking for deeper insights into digital advertising and ad tech? This one is for you. It's tailored for industry professionals looking to deepen their understanding of complex marketing technologies. With in-depth interviews, each episode tackles topics like AI in marketing, ad fraud prevention, data privacy regulations, and the evolving media landscape. With hosts with years of ad tech experience, the podcast offers both tactical advice and strategic insights into how technology is shaping the future of advertising and media.


Behind the Numbers: eMarketer Podcast
Hosted by eMarketer analysts, this data-driven series breaks down the biggest trends in digital marketing, media, and tech. The hosts tackle everything from social media shifts to the latest in e-commerce and CTV, with data-driven insights to back it up. It’s snappy, informative, and perfect for marketers who want a daily dose of what’s driving the digital economy. If you want to stay in the loop on industry trends without all the fluff, this one’s for you!


The Digiday Podcast
Digiday’s podcast takes a deep dive into the latest digital media and features interviews with industry leaders. It covers topics such as ad tech, privacy regulations, and how companies adjust to the challenges of a data-driven advertising world.

The WARC Podcast
This podcast delivers expert-level insights into marketing effectiveness, innovation, and strategy. With engaging discussions led by industry professionals and WARC editors, each episode dives into topics like the future of media, consumer behavior shifts, and the latest trends driving marketing success. It’s an invaluable resource for marketers looking to sharpen their strategies with data-driven insights and actionable advice from leading brands and agencies. Whether you’re interested in global campaigns or cutting-edge marketing techniques, this podcast keeps you informed on what works—and what’s next.

The MadTech Podcast
by ExchangeWire
Focusing on the intersection of media, advertising, and technology, this podcast explores how advancements like AI, programmatic advertising, and customer data reshape the marketing landscape. Their episodes on AI’s role in media buying have been particularly insightful around here.

The Big Story
This podcast from AdExchanger is a go-to for anyone looking to keep a pulse on ad tech and programmatic advertising. It features industry experts discussing key trends such as privacy challenges, new programmatic opportunities, and how ad tech is evolving in a cookie-less world. They are keeping an especially close eye on recent Google regulatory developments.

Ad Age Marketer’s Brief
The Ad Age Marketer's Brief podcast provides a weekly snapshot of the latest trends and key stories in marketing. Hosted by E.J. Schultz and Adrianne Pasquarelli, it features interviews with industry leaders and dives into topics like brand strategy, consumer behavior, and advertising trends. It’s perfect for marketers wanting quick insights into major campaigns and marketing innovations from top brands. Recent episodes cover everything from loyalty programs to product launches and digital platform strategies.


ANA Marketing Futures Podcast
Hosted by Michael Berberich and our friends at the Association of National Advertisers (ANA), this podcast dives into the innovations shaping the future of marketing. With a focus on cutting-edge topics like retail media networks, AI’s growing role in customer engagement, and the future of programmatic advertising, the podcast offers in-depth discussions on how technology transforms how brands connect with consumers. Each episode features thought leaders and industry experts sharing practical insights on emerging trends such as blockchain, the metaverse, and the evolving data privacy landscape. It’s a must-listen for marketers looking to stay ahead in a rapidly changing digital world, providing visionary ideas and actionable advice.


Commerce Collective

Flywheel Digital’s podcast explores what they know best: e-commerce, retail media, and digital transformation. Featuring insightful conversations with industry leaders, the podcast covers strategies and trends shaping commerce's future, from optimizing online marketplaces to navigating the complexities of retail media networks. Whether you’re an e-commerce veteran or just looking to stay in the loop, Commerce Collective offers valuable tips and expert perspectives to help you thrive in a rapidly evolving corner of the ad universe.

ADTechGod Pod
What sets the AdTechGod Pod apart is its engaging exploration of both the technical aspects of ad tech and the human stories behind them. With a strong focus on personal stories, industry insights, and the career journeys of its guests, this podcast helps listeners understand the intricacies of the ad tech world and its impacts on the broader ecosystem. Each episode covers a broad range of topics, from programmatic advertising and data privacy to the future of digital media and the challenges faced by marketing professionals in a rapidly evolving space.

Yeah, That's Probably an Ad
Adweek’s go-to podcast for anyone who wants to stay on top of the latest in media, marketing, and creativity. Hosted by Adweek's editors, the show covers everything from groundbreaking advertising campaigns to the trends shaping the industry's future. With insightful conversations featuring industry leaders and cultural influencers, each episode looks at the topics everyone in the marketing world is buzzing about. Whether you’re into media innovation, brand strategy, or pop culture, this podcast offers a sharp and engaging take on the stories driving the advertising landscape.

The Current Podcast
This podcast, published by The Trade Desk, delivers fast-paced insights on the latest trends shaping digital advertising and media. Each episode brings in industry leaders to explore how technology is revolutionizing how brands reach consumers, with topics ranging from connected TV to retail media. Quick, insightful, and relevant—this podcast keeps you plugged into what’s driving the future of media.

The Drum Network Podcasts
The Drum offers a series of podcasts, including Anatomy of an Ad, TV Talks, and The Drum Network, all designed to focus on specific corners of the advertising universe. dives deep into the changes happening in digital advertising and technology. They frequently discuss how ad tech is evolving to meet the demands of a privacy-focused world, making it a valuable listen for those focused on the future of the digital ecosystem.

The Digital Deep Dive Podcast
Hosted by Aaron Conant
The Digital Deep Dive Podcast tackles e-commerce, retail media, and digital transformation topics. It’s a great podcast for marketers and advertisers exploring how digital-first strategies shape the industry's future.


Retail Remix
Retail Remix is another go-to podcast for staying ahead of retail media networks, e-commerce trends, and digital innovation. Hosted by industry insiders, it breaks down how brands can fine-tune their strategies to thrive in today’s fast-paced market. A recent episode dives into how brands optimize retail media strategies to stay competitive in a constantly shifting landscape. Whether you're looking to boost your digital game or keep up with retail’s rapid evolution, Retail Remix has the insights and practical tips you need to succeed, all served with a fresh, conversational vibe.
