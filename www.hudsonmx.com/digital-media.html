BuyerAssist for Digital 

Peace of mind and real-time cross-platform, cross-channel insight—however and wherever you choose to buy.

Your custom-configurable central hub for any and all digital buys, BuyerAssist simplifies digital direct workflows and syncs up with all your other buying platforms in real time. Whether you “push” to or “pull” from your favorite 3rd party buying tools, you’ll lighten administrative burdens, eliminate manual data entry, and gain real-time cross-platform, cross-channel visibility.

 

Unlocking efficiency, accuracy, and innovation for digital media teams.

  • Plan & forecast for programmatic, search, social and digital direct in real time—within a single workflow.

    • Planned budget updates

    • Actualization

    • Reconciliation

    • Purchase order & tracking

    • Approvals

    • Maintenance of billable and payable details, including margin.

    • Pacing alerts

  • Actualize investments in DSPs and other data sources across workflows, via automated APIs.

    • Leverage two-way integrations between Hudson MX, and partners including Google, Bing, Facebook, Innovid, Google Campaign Manager, Sizmek, DV360, and DCM to enable seamless pushing of campaigns and revisions to the ad server, and an automatic, real-time view of delivery data in the buying platform.

    • Leverage automated performance reporting for integrated vendors via our flexible API.

  • Employ advanced strategies and adopt new strategies without barriers.

    • Adapt new data sources and surface insights from end to end.

    • Use automated data aggregation to create cross-medium reports in minutes.

    • Update vendor rates in real-time, across platforms for better deal management.

    • Employ new strategies and create custom workflows for emerging mediums and platforms with minimal ramp-up effort required.
